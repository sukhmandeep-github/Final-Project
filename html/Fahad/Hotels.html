One King West Hotel & Residence

1 King Street West, Toronto, Ontario M5H 1A1 Canada

Built in 1914, this downtown Toronto hotel blends historic charm and elegance with modern conveniences. St.Lawrence Market and the Hockey Hall of Fame are less than 10 minutes' walk away.

An airy restaurant/bar features floor-to-ceiling windows. Other amenities include a rooftop indoor pool, a sauna and a fitness room. Meeting rooms and a 24-hour business centre are available. Parking is offered for a fee.

Sheraton Centre Toronto Hotel

123 Queen Street West, Toronto, Ontario M5H 2M9 Canada

Located in the centre of downtown Toronto, this 4-star Sheraton is located across the street from Nathan Phillips Square, a 5-minute walk from CF Toronto Eaton Centre. Upscale amenities include an indoor/outdoor heated pool, modern gym, and renovated 43rd floor Club Lounge.

Simple rooms come with flat-screen TVs and free Wi-Fi, plus minifridges, and tea and coffeemakers. Microwaves are available on request. Children age 17 and under stay at no charge with an adult.

There's a gym, and parking and coin-operated laundry facilities are available.
